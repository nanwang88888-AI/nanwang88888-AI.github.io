Research Focus

What I Research

My research spans computational biology, data science, and emerging technologies. I develop algorithms and systems for genomic data analysis, structural annotation, and machine learning-based prediction models.

Recent work focuses on Generative AI, Blockchain Technology, and FinTech — exploring how LLMs and decentralized systems transform scientific discovery and computing.

Introduction

About

Dr. Nan Wang is an Associate Professor of Computer Science at William Paterson University with over two decades of academic and research experience spanning computational biology, bioinformatics, machine learning, blockchain technology, and generative AI.

Her interdisciplinary work bridges data science and life sciences, with publications in leading venues including Nature Scientific Reports, BMC Genomics, and Nucleic Acids Research. She is passionate about developing the next generation of computer scientists through innovative curriculum design and mentorship.

Dr. Wang has secured over $22 million in funded research grants from agencies including NSF, USDA, NOAA, and USAID, and has recently co-authored a textbook on Blockchain with a Python programming book in preparation.
